The Wonderful World of Wes Anderson Filmography

The Wonderful World of Wes Anderson Filmography was my very first attempt at making a web-based interactive map using Javascript and Leaflet. This interactive is a proportional symbol map that details the filmimg location of each Wes Anderson movie as well as the estimated movie budget indicated by the proportional symbol.
